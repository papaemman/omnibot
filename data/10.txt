https://www.aclcf.org/news/award-by-the-annual-sustainability-summit/ | AWARD BY THE ANNUAL SUSTAINABILITY SUMMIT The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation received a distinction for the donation of 600.000 reusable water bottles for personal use to primary school students, as a practice with significant social impact and an innovation, which stood out in the midst of the pandemic in Greece. The award ceremony was held on October 13, 2020 at the “Annual Sustainability Summit” event, by MBA International of the Athens University of Economics and Business in collaboration with the Center for Sustainability and Excellence (CSE).
