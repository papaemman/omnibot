https://www.aclcf.org/about-en/the-foundation/ | The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation was established in 2015 in order to act as a catalyst for important issues concerning Greece and Hellenism. Its action focuses on the protection of the marine environment, the support of employment and micro-entrepreneurship as well as the fostering of initiatives, which defend collective interest and enhance transparency. The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation designs and implements its own programs, while at the same time supporting programs and actions of other bodies in its areas of focus. Believing in the value of cooperation, it undertakes joint actions with non-profit organizations, experts from all areas and other funders to bring significant results and leave the greatest footprint. Making use of all available resources and an extensive network of partners, the Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation studies in depth its areas of focus, identifies best practices and harnesses innovative solutions globally. Firmly believing in the ability of individuals to make a difference beginning from their own communities, it supports efforts that have a direct impact on local societies and seek to bring about change, starting from the little things.
