The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation collaborates with the Ministry of Culture and Sports to develop actions in areas related to the protection of the natural and cultural environment, the cultivation of environmental awareness and the elaboration of awareness-raising training programs in the protection of the monumental reserve. Within the framework of the three-year Memorandum of Cooperation, that was signed in September 2021, the Foundation coordinates clean-up actions in areas of special historical importance, educational programs and environmental awareness-raising activities.
