Chloe Maria Laskaridis
Board member
Chloe Laskaridis holds a B.A. in History and International Relations from the University of Reading, in the UK. She also holds a post-graduate degree in War Studies (MA) from King’s College in London and a post-graduate degree in Human Resource Management (MSc) from the London School of Economics.
Chloe has served as the Director of Corporate Development at Lampsa Hellenic Hotels SA since 2008. She is also a board member of The People’s Trust.
