Through a wide range of actions and synergies, the Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation highlights culture as one of its priorities. Protection and development of monuments and selective interventions in the public space are the basic outline of the Foundation's planning for the preservation of cultural heritage.




Tatoi
The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation undertook the financing of a study for the evaluation and updating of the proposals for the development, exploitation and management of the Former Royal Estate in Tatoi, with the aim of ensuring the viability of the historic site under the supervision of the Ministry of Culture and Sports.

Omonoia Fountain
Participation in the renovation of the emblematic Omonoia square, by supporting the construction of a new impressive fountain system.


Mon Repos
The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation participates in the renovation, protection and promotion of the Mon Repos Park, contributing financially to the cost of completing a study and to embellishment activities of the Park.
