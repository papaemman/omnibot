Peggy Xirotagarou
Deputy Director
Peggy has worked for a decade in the advertising sector for large agencies such as SPOT THOMPSON and ADEL, starting as an Account Executive and continuing as a Client Service Director. She then moved on to the client’s side as a Marketing and Public Relations Manager for large multinational companies such as Estee Lauder Hellas and Zepter Hellas. In 2007 she created her own event planning company, Planit Cool. She has studied Marketing, Advertising and Public Relations at Eurocentre College International and St George College.
