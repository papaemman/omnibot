Nikos Mysirlakis
Program Manager  
Nikos is a graduate of the Department of Environmental Engineering of the School of Engineering at the University of Patras and is currently completing his MSc in Environmental, Disasters and Crises Management Strategies from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, with a specialization in Environmental Education. He holds a certificate of Adult Education from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. He has actively volunteered for various NGO’s focusing on education and environmental protection. He has worked as an Environmental Educator at private organizations and has co-authored an educational guide on the adaptation to Climate Change.
