Evi Lazou – Laskaridis
Chairman
Evi has broad experience in marketing and operations, and has served as Executive Vice President Marketing for Hyatt Regency Hotels and Tourism Hellas S.A. She holds a BA Magna Cum Laude with Honors in Organizational Behavior and French Civilisation from Brown University, Providence, RI and studied interior design at The New School, Parsons School of Design in New York.
Evi is a founding partner and Chairman of the board of The People’s Trust, an organisation that provides business development services and micro-funding to start-ups and small, existing businesses in Greece. She is also one of the founding partners of Greenwood Place, a community of strategic philanthropists.
Originally from Greece, she currently lives in London with her partner and young son.
