The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation in collaboration with pod.gr is creating "Eco – Agents", a series of podcasts promoting information and raising awareness on environmental protection.




The podcast features discussions with people active in the cycle of environmental protection. Organizations, activists, environmental experts, fishermen, scientists, children and ordinary citizens, all become “agents” dedicated to saving nature.
