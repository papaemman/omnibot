EDUCATION


The Athanasios C. Laskaridis Charitable Foundation supports the sector of education in many ways, implementing educational programs on the environment and enhancing educational activities of other bodies. Scholarships, funding of research activities and support of tried and tested projects frame the core of the Foundation’s educational programs, which aims to inform and raise awareness on environmental issues.
